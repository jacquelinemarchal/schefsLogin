Ambassador Town Hall

Sunday, 09/27/2020 @ 5 pm EST

This open town hall is for anyone interested in the ambassador program to gather (via Zoom) with the Schefs team (four undergrad students!) and discuss how we can grow this community of young thinkers and learners. You’ll learn more about our plan for the program as well as have the opportunity to share your own thoughts. Schefs is all about collaboration: it is by and for students to exchange ideas, passions, and knowledge with one another. So as we grow, we want to make our internal plans as open-source, participatory, and inclusive as possible.

Our mission is to create spaces for college students to come together in live conversation on topics they care about. We need your help to continue to create deeper and non-hierarchical ways for us students to meet friends and collaborators and inspire one another through a community that values knowledge, passion, contribution, diverse perspectives, and collective learning.

Stop by, say hello, and learn more about the ambassador program as we create it together!
