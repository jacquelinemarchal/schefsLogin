About Sign In

We are a platform for college students to facilitate and participate in themed conversations over a meal on topics of their choice, using “mealtime” as a vehicle for community, dialogue, and knowledge exchange.

We create micro-think tanks for students to share/learn from one another, physically and virtually, and to harness the collective intelligence of our generation.

If you can have a conversation about something you’re either a master at or simply curious about, you can host a Schefs event.

1. Sign up to host
2. Fill out an event proposal.
3. Meet and discuss with 7 other college students with similar interests.
